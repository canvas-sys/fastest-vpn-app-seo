## FAQ

**Does fastest vpn app keep logs?**  
The review standard is a **No-Logs policy**. If a provider’s policy is vague, it’s flagged.

**Will it slow down my internet?**  
Any VPN adds overhead. The point here is **high speed** + **stable connection**—especially under load and on distant routes.

**What happens if the VPN drops?**  
A **Kill Switch** (when supported) blocks traffic to prevent accidental exposure.

**Is encryption strong enough for public Wi‑Fi?**  
Yes—**AES-256 encryption** is the baseline called out in the review criteria.
